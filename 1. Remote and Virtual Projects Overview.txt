# 1. Remote and Virtual Projects Overview
-----------------------------------------

* What is different about remote projects?
------------------------------------------
Balance autonomy and independence with the right amount of oversight


* Reduce Uncertainty with Structure
-----------------------------------
. Set times for meetings

. Specific collaboration tools

. Expectations for one-on-ones and stand-ups


* Remote projects require more TRANSPARENCY
-------------------------------------------
. You can't see what your team is doing

. You can't see if the member of your team are confusing or worried

. The PM needs to model all the improving communication
